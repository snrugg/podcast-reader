Architecture Notes

This player runs Kokoro TTS — an 82M parameter neural text-to-speech model — entirely in your browser. No servers, no API keys, no data leaves your device.

  • kokoro-js loads the ONNX model from HuggingFace (~150MB, cached after first load)
  • Inference runs via WebGPU (Chrome 113+) with automatic WebAssembly fallback
  • Text is chunked into sentences and streamed — audio plays as each chunk finishes
  • Generated audio is WAV PCM at 24kHz — download to get broadcast-quality files
  • Ranked just behind ElevenLabs in TTS Arena quality benchmarks

Requires a modern browser (Chrome/Edge recommended for WebGPU). First load downloads ~150MB of model weights. Subsequent visits use cached weights.
